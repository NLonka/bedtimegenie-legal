Refund Policy

BedtimeGenie Pro  ·  Last updated: May 2025

Short version: All purchases are processed by Apple App Store or Google Play. Refunds are handled directly by those platforms — we do not process payments ourselves.

1. How Purchases Work

BedtimeGenie Pro offers subscriptions (Monthly, Annual) and a one-time Lifetime purchase. All transactions are processed securely through:

  • Apple App Store — for iOS and iPadOS users
  • Google Play Store — for Android users

We do not directly handle payment information. Billing is managed entirely by Apple or Google.

2. How to Request a Refund

🍎 Apple App Store

Visit reportaproblem.apple.com, sign in with your Apple ID, find the BedtimeGenie Pro purchase, and select "Request a Refund". Apple typically responds within a few days.

🤖 Google Play Store

Open the Google Play Store app → tap your profile icon → Payments & subscriptions → Budget & history → find the purchase and tap "Refund". Alternatively visit play.google.com/store/account/subscriptions.

3. Subscriptions

Subscriptions auto-renew unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the renewal date. To cancel:

  • iOS: Settings → [Your Name] → Subscriptions → BedtimeGenie Pro → Cancel
  • Android: Google Play → Subscriptions → BedtimeGenie Pro → Cancel

Cancelling stops future charges but does not automatically generate a refund for the current billing period. Contact Apple or Google for refund requests.

4. Lifetime Purchase

The Lifetime plan is a one-time purchase. If you experience a technical issue that prevents you from using Premium features, please contact us at bedtimegenie@gmail.com before requesting a refund, and we will do our best to resolve the issue.

5. Contact Us

If you have a billing question we can help with, email us at bedtimegenie@gmail.com.
